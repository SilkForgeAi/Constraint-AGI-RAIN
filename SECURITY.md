Security Policy

Rain is designed for sovereign-grade safety and auditability. This document describes how critical assets are protected.


Air-Gapped Safety Vault

Core logic and the full 93% capability build are not present in this repository. They are held in an Air-Gapped Safety Vault: isolated from public networks, with access controlled by physical and procedural safeguards. The public repository contains architecture documentation, capability specifications, sales and positioning material, and deployment references—not the executable core or safety-critical implementation.


Sovereign-Grade Audit

Access to the 93% build and the contents of the Air-Gapped Safety Vault requires a Sovereign-Grade Audit. That process includes identity verification, purpose-of-use review, and compliance with our security and licensing terms. No distribution of core logic or full build is made without completion of this process.


Reporting Security Issues

If you believe you have found a security issue in the materials that are publicly available (e.g. documentation or specifications), please report it responsibly to aaron@vexaai.app. Do not disclose details publicly until we have had a chance to assess and respond.
